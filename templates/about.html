From a Seed of Passion to a Flourishing Bouquet:

The Fingrace Flowers Story

It all began in 2021, amidst the uncertain bloom of a global pandemic. In a small, humble room tucked away in Kuala Lumpur, Fingrace Flowers sprouted from a single seed of passion. Armed with a mere RM1500 and a heart brimming with love for nature's artistry, we dared to dream of bringing joy through vibrant blooms.

Our journey began on the bustling avenues of e-commerce. Shopee, Lazada, and Tik Tok - these digital marketplaces became our fertile ground, where we nurtured connections and blossomed alongside our customers' celebrations. With every carefully crafted bouquet, every delicate bud delivered, Fingrace Flowers earned its name, petal by petal.

As our roots grew deeper, our vision bloomed. The cramped room, once a haven for creativity, could no longer contain our burgeoning dreams. It was time to spread our wings, to dance in the sunlight of a larger space. So, we ventured into Setapak, our small team of passionate individuals finding a new home in a cozy warehouse.

Today, Fingrace Flowers stands tall, no longer a seedling in a cramped room, but a thriving bouquet in its own right. We are a testament to the power of passion and perseverance, a testament that even the smallest beginnings can blossom into something beautiful. From those initial online orders to our current warehouse, we've journeyed through challenges and celebrations, weaving stories of joy with every bloom.

But our journey is far from over. We continue to strive, to create, to cultivate connections that go beyond mere transactions. Fingrace Flowers is not just a business; it's a canvas where we paint smiles with petals, a stage where love and joy take center stage.

So, the next time you hold a Fingrace bouquet, know that you're not just holding flowers, but a piece of our story. A story of resilience, of growth, and a passion that continues to bloom, one fragrant bloom at a time.
